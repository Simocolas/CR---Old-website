Should the soffit, fascia, and eavestrough colours be the same or different?

The modern trend is for the soffit, fascia, and trough to all be the same colour. This creates an elegant look compared to the classic option of white soffit and differently coloured fascia and trough.

What Type of Siding Should I Get?

It’s important to discuss your preferences and vision with an experienced contractor who knows how to get you the look you want. We can help you determine what’s best for your home.

How do I maintain my siding?

Your siding and soffit should be washed periodically by simply rinsing your home down with your garden hose, paying special attention to areas that are not exposed to rain. 

Can I attach things to my siding?

If you want to boost the curb appeal of your home by hanging items on the side, such as window boxes or holiday decorations, the most important thing you must know is that you should never drill a hole into your siding. This can leave room for moisture and allow mold to grow.

The Exterior Of Your Home Is Placed For Your Safety

While appearances are important, the functionality and safety of your property is our biggest concern. We’ve served the Calgary area for many years, and we’ve seen what happens when the exterior of a home is neglected. Clogged or damaged gutters can lead to water damage that is costly to repair, and old siding may fail to protect the structure from wind, rain and other elements of the weather. This is why we’re committed to offering affordable exterior care services to all Calgarians. From new installations to repairs and replacements, we have the skill, resources and knowledge to get your exterior in line with your vision for the property.
